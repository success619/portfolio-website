About me

Overview

I am a Senior Systems Architect and Full-Stack Engineer dedicated to building high-concurrency, resilient digital ecosystems. My core technical arsenal spans TypeScript, JavaScript (ES2026+), Python, PHP, and C++. I specialize in architecting sophisticated, decoupled solutions using Next.js 15, React, Node.js, and Django, while maintaining deep proficiency in Vue 3, Angular, and Three.js for immersive, high-fidelity user experiences.

I operate with a 'Secure-by-Design' philosophy, integrating advanced defensive programming into every layer of the stack. My expertise covers Vulnerability Assessment, OWASP Top 10 mitigation, and active penetration testing. I specialize in neutralizing complex threats like SQL/NoSQL injections and XSS, ensuring data integrity through schema-level validation (Zod) and robust Identity and Access Management (IAM).

I thrive on engineering Real-Time Event-Driven Architectures using Socket.io and Redis, supported by scalable database strategies involving PostgreSQL (utilizing UUIDs and JSONB for elasticity). My background in Data Analysis and Network Engineering allows me to optimize server-side performance and cloud infrastructure—specifically within AWS (S3, EC2, Lambda) and modern CI/CD pipelines.

As a Technologically Agile Lead, I translate complex business requirements into efficient, scalable, and user-centric products. I don’t just write code; I build solutions that drive business success. Let’s collaborate to engineer your next breakthrough.
